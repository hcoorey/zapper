About the Zapper

The Zapper is a revolutionary medical device that disintegrates needles with ease. It is cost effective, safe, and environmentally friendly, making it the perfect choice for hospitals and healthcare facilities worldwide.

Features

Cost Effective

The Zapper is a third of the price of other needle disintegration devices, saving your facility money without sacrificing quality.

Safety

The Zapper vaporizes 99.7% of mass instantly, ensuring the safest possible disposal of needles and other sharp objects.

Environmental Friendliness

The Zapper is environmentally friendly, using a process that produces zero waste and eliminates the need for incineration or other harmful disposal methods.
